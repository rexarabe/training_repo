14.7 Other Desktop Topics
One interesting characteristic of the Linux desktop envrionment is that you can genreally choose which pieces you want to use and stop using the ones that you dislike.
For a survey of many of the dektop projects, have a look at the mailing lists and project links for the various projects at http://www.freedesktop.org/. Elsewhere, you'll find other desktop projects, such as Ayatana, Unity and Mir.
Another major development in the Linux desktop is the Chomium OS open source porject and its Google Chrome OS counterpart found on Chromebook PCs.
This is a Linux system that use much of the desktop techonology described in this chapter but is centered around the Chromium/Chrome web browsers.
Much of what's found on a traditonal desktop has been stripped away in Chrome OS.
